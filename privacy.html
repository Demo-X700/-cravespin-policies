CraveSpin Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 11, 2025

Introduction

CraveSpin ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Email Address: When you create an account (optional)
  • Username: For account identification
  • Profile Preferences: Meal times, dietary restrictions, ingredient preferences

App Activity Data

  • Recipe Searches: What recipes you search for
  • App Interactions: Features you use, recipes you view
  • User-Generated Content: Comments, reviews, and ratings you post
  • Favorites: Recipes you save

Technical Information

  • Device IDs: For app functionality and analytics
  • Crash Logs: To improve app stability
  • Performance Data: To optimize app performance

How We Use Your Information

App Functionality

  • Provide personalized recipe recommendations
  • Save your preferences and favorites
  • Enable account features (optional)
  • Display your comments and reviews

Analytics and Improvement

  • Monitor app performance and fix bugs
  • Understand how users interact with features
  • Improve recipe recommendations

Information Sharing

Third-Party Services

We share data with these service providers:

  • Firebase (Google): User authentication, database, analytics
  • FatSecret API: Recipe data and nutritional information
  • Expo/React Native: App performance and crash reporting

We Do Not

  • Sell your personal information
  • Share data for advertising purposes
  • Use your data for marketing communications

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • All data transmission is encrypted (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Firebase provides enterprise-grade security
  • Access to user data is restricted and monitored

Your Rights and Choices

Account Data

  • Access: View your account information in the app
  • Update: Modify your preferences and profile
  • Delete: Request account deletion (see our Data Deletion Policy)

Optional Features

  • Creating an account is optional
  • You can use core features without signing up
  • You can delete comments and reviews you've posted

Children's Privacy

CraveSpin is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

Data Retention

  • Account Data: Retained until account deletion
  • Comments/Reviews: Retained for 30 days after account deletion
  • Analytics Data: Aggregated data may be retained indefinitely
  • Crash Logs: Automatically deleted after 90 days

International Users

CraveSpin is operated from Australia. By using our app, you consent to the transfer and processing of your information in Australia and other countries where our service providers operate.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes through the app or by email. Your continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or want to exercise your rights:

  • Email: CraveSpin@gmail.com
  • Subject Line: Privacy Policy Inquiry
  • Response Time: Within 7 business days

Data Deletion: For account deletion requests, see our Data Deletion Policy
