CraveSpin - Account and Data Deletion

How to Delete Your CraveSpin Account

To request deletion of your CraveSpin account and associated data, please follow these steps:

Step 1: Contact Us

Send an email to support@cravespin.app with the subject line "Account Deletion Request"

Step 2: Include Required Information

In your email, please include:

  • The email address associated with your CraveSpin account
  • Your username (if different from email)
  • Confirmation that you want to permanently delete your account

Step 3: Verification

We will verify your identity and process your request within 7 business days.

What Data Gets Deleted

When you request account deletion, we will permanently remove:

✅ Account Information

  • Email address
  • Username
  • Profile preferences
  • Meal time settings

✅ User-Generated Content

  • Recipe comments
  • Recipe reviews and ratings
  • Favorite recipes list
  • Ingredient preferences and exclusions

✅ App Activity Data

  • Search history
  • Recipe interaction data
  • App usage analytics tied to your account

Data Retention

  • Immediate deletion: Account credentials and personal preferences
  • 30-day retention: Comments and reviews (to maintain recipe integrity for other users)
  • Anonymous data: Some aggregated, non-identifiable usage statistics may be retained for app improvement

Important Notes

  • Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone
  • You will lose access to all saved preferences and favorites
  • Any comments or reviews will be anonymized after 30 days
  • You can create a new account at any time

Contact Information

For account deletion requests or questions:

  • Email: support@cravespin.app
  • Response time: Within 7 business days
  • Developer: DemoX700
